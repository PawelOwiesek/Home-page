About the course.

 I came across the YouCode course by accident while looking for information about C#. This is another language that interested me and I will definitely want to explore it, but it's already in a Dutch university after completing the current course. YouCode is a front-end oriented course. It coversHTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bem convention, GitHub, React, Jeson format and some additional issues that a good frontend developer should know. I started the course on November 30, 2022. It is an online course. It consists of several chapters, each of them ends with homework and the whole course is crowned with group work.
